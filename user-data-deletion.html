User Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these instructions:

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Log in to your Pawlsar account.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings page.
  3. Select the option to delete your account.
  4. Confirm your decision by entering your password and clicking the delete button.

For assistance, please contact our support team at support@pawlsar.com.
